About the project


Project 1000

The project entails the purchase of JAMB registration forms and the development of online tutorials for 1000 students towards the examination in April, 2024.


The project is anticipated to be completed within the next 3-4 months, matching the released registration and examination dates from JAMB.

How to Support


Support a Child
